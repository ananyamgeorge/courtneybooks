How it works

  1. Open the chat. Tap “Chat on WhatsApp”, or find Courtney on Telegram. Say hi.
  2. Tell her what you want. The sport, your city or area, and when. Like “padel in Bandra tomorrow 7pm”.
  3. She searches all three apps at once. Hudle, KheloMore and Playo together, and sends back the open courts near you with name, price, rating and distance.
  4. You pick one and book. Tap the link, then book and pay on the court’s own page. Courtney finds it; the booking is yours to make.
